NP Membership Benefits

Non-profit organizations enjoy a fee-free membership that grants access to exclusive networking events, community resources, and free workshops.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Bronze members receive a listing in our directory, monthly newsletters, and discounted rates for events and workshops.

Silver Membership Benefits

Silver members benefit from enhanced directory visibility, priority invitations to special events, and promotional support through our social media channels.

Gold Membership Benefits

Gold members receive premium listings, exclusive networking opportunities, featured spotlights on our website, and complimentary advertising packages.
