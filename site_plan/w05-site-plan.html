Hong Kong Cultural Experience

Site Name: Hong Kong Cultural Experience

Reason for Name: This name captures the unique blend of modern vibrancy and rich tradition that defines Hong Kong. It invites visitors to explore its cultural heritage through a concise, easy-to-navigate website.

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a brief yet engaging introduction to the cultural highlights of Hong Kong. The content will focus on key aspects such as traditional festivals, authentic local foods, and iconic landmarks. The simple design—with only a few pages—ensures that visitors can quickly access and enjoy the essential information about Hong Kong’s vibrant culture.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: As a tourist interested in cultural traditions, I want to know which festivals and events best showcase Hong Kong’s heritage.
  • Scenario 2: As a food enthusiast, I need to quickly find recommendations for authentic street food and traditional dining spots in Hong Kong.

Color Schema

Primary Color: #E60012 – Applied to headings, accent elements, and key call-to-action buttons.

Secondary Color: #FFFFFF – Used as the background color to provide a clean contrast with the primary color.

Typography

Font Selected: Roboto

Roboto will be used throughout the site for both headings and body text to maintain a modern, clean, and easily readable design.
